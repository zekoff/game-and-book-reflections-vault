Acclaimed fishing game billed as a relaxing and beautiful experience. It delivers. There is some light progression with buying new lures, reels, boats, and fishing spots. My favorite part so far has been filling out my Pokedex of fish. I have come back to this several times interspersed with other games just to snag a few more fish. The call of collecting the cash resource is strong, but the desire to see and catch all the fish varieties is stronger.
