Incredible rebirth of the immersive sim genre. It is set in grim "whalepunk" world, an alternate-universe version of Victorian London. The Blink power is one of its signatures. The game is one of the few stealth games where, in the course of a normal playthrough, I would fight it out anytime I was discovered rather than load from a save point.
