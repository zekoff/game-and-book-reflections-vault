Playdate game that thoroughly integrates the crank into the game.

Playing this game, holding the Playdate and spinning the crank, I felt like I was holding a little puzzle box. Turning gears inside this device and then closing it up again when I was done playing. This game is extraordinarily well matched with the form factor of the device.

Also, a mercifully short game.
