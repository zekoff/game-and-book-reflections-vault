Competent match-3 game. It injects variety by providing a slightly-different Challenge goal in each stage. They provide an alternate target even though most of them aren’t required. New tweaks are also slowly introduced over the course of the story mode.

That story is a not-so-thinly veiled cut at an Amazon fulfillment center. An oft-used crutch in current games is to make smug but shallow commentary about some aspect of capitalism. It’s tiresome after seeing it in many games. Although it actually fits this game well, the repetition is grating. This has become so pervasive that it’s like a hip version of the fantasy or sci-fi themes: slap it onto your game and your work is done.
