A new JRPG franchise that delivered. Path actions were the "one new thing" and they delivered on the premise of each character having something different outside of combat as well as within. Each character was the traditional representation of their class. The warrior was a grizzled veteran. The cleric was a naive member of the clergy. The rogue was morally-grey with ennui deep in his bones.
