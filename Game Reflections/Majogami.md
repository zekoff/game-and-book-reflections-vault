Demo. Inti Creates. 2D action game. Signature feature is a move that cuts instantly to an enemy in a vertical, diagonal, or horizontal aspect. Sometimes the angle must be matched to an enemy weakness. This doesn't break new ground. The camera is very close to the player and dissuades any kind of forethought in how to approach stages. Perhaps they could be memorized for speed runs. But the moveset is not flexible enough to separate average play from excellent play. The paper craft window dressing is pleasant but thin.
