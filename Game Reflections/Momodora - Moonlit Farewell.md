Played about an hour. Not for me. There was an early nod towards a relationship system. This implies that the characters have a place of importance in the game's design. I won't care about these characters so that was an early data point. The first ability unlocked allowed me to slash vines. The second allowed me to sprint. If double jump is retained as a big reveal three or more ability unlocks in, it's unlikely the game will later floor me with its unique traversal and combat options. The mechanical augmentations are important to me. The idea of new abilities recontextualizing the map is the hallmark of a strong metroidvania. It takes increasingly more to stand out now.
