My first exposure was an arcade coin-op machine. I later got it somehow as a downloadable Xbox game. Incredible animations. Solid gameplay, of which the grenade is really the moment-to-moment thing that stands out the most. The feeling of hopping into a Metal Slug is also unforgettable. This was the spiritual basis for [Mech Rescue](https://zekoff.com/mech-rescue).
