Still a fun read -- doesn’t hit me as hard as it did many years ago.

There’s a sequence where computer techs through the ages monitor grand thinking machines (in fact, the whole galactic-stake arc depends on it). This must certainly have been cribbed from [Asimov’s “The Last Question”](https://users.ece.cmu.edu/~gamvrosi/thelastq.html).
