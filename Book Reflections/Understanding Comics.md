Insightful for its observations about how the brain processes things in this unique medium. Makes a case that comics are special because of the way they combine pictures and words. Explores the different ways to mark time, the use of “closure” (the empty space between panels), the continuum or “picture plane” of realism, iconography, words which conjure meaning, symbols. Also makes some observations about how comics fit into or work in the context of all forms of media. Postulates that all creators go through the process in their work of
1. Idea/Purpose 
2. Form
3. Idiom
4. Structure
5. Craft
6. Surface
